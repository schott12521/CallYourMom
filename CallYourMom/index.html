APP NAME
Call Your Mother
link to .apk
link to source zip

In a world full of distractions that could stop a user from calling their mother,
Call Your Mom allows users to create recurring reminders for users to call the ones close to them.
A user can set the amount of time in between reminders, and if they decide to call
before the next reminder, then the reminder is pushed back. Call Your Mom is great for college kids looking to be
reminded (or nagged!) when they should call their mom.
